GET TO KNOW ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Health Information Systems Nigeria is Nigeria's leading Health Informatics Institution. HISP Nigeria was established in 2006 by a group of Social Scientists, Information Technology, and Health Information technology professionals. HISP was set up to drive the paradigm shift in health data and information management. Moving it from a data collection for reporting to information for functional planning and management approach.

HISP works to bring equity and evidence to the Nigerian health systems space. Providing technical support for the Federal Ministry of Health and all partners and standing at the vanguard of health information management innovations and solutions construction.

HISP is active and visible member of the global HISP Network, we are the official academy partner for Nigeria and have the deepest expertise and experience in delivering Health Information Management Systems content in Nigeria.

OUR MISSION

HISP Nigeria will provide and support Health System Strengthening (HSS), innovations and solutions for Health and other developmental programs Technical advise and assistance for appropriate ICT soft and hardware for health information systems. Technical advice and guidance for information needs assessment, performance assessment and program evaluations. Design, configure, roll out and manage information systems.

OUR VISSION

To be the most innovative, visible and relevant health informatics organization in Nigeria. Producing the highest quality evidence-based services, solutions and products. Covering a broad field of services; primary in health informatics but also in the larger development space these will include monitoring and evaluation of products for action in developmental planning and management. Thus, ensuring that there is equity in the allocation of resources for all communities.

OUR EXPERTISE

Database Design and Deployment

At HISP Nigeria, we design our databases with the most reliable Database Management Systems that provide tools to create, use and maintain or manage the databases. Relational Databases are extensively used in the building of reliable, Enterprise Relationship Planning (ERP) Software solutions. Most of our Digital Solutions are deployed to production with highly scalable relational databases

Database Management

Our team has experts that have more than 8 years cognate experience as Database Administrators who maintain and manage our databases in production environments and are sure to deliver on the job. Our reliable expertise provides a stable and flexible management of application resources and databases in production. Our data-driven DHIS2 applications are thus reliable solutions.

Monitoring and Evaluation

Our Donors can monitor and evaluate all our assessed activities towards a particular project. Monitoring involves the continuous assessment of programs based on early detailed information on the progress or delay of the ongoing assessed activities to improve management of outputs, outcomes and impact of products. Evaluation involves the examination of the relevance, effectiveness, efficiency and impact of activities in the light of specified objectives.

DHIS2 Implementation and Technical Assistance

Leveraging on our staff mix of professionals from the health and non-health sector we have extensive experience in both direct implementation and technical assistance for health service delivery. Our technical assistance activities include DHIS2 Integration, DHIS2 Application development, DHIS2 Server Administration, DHIS2 Academy and Specialized Training, DHIS2 Customization and support and Health Management Information System (HMIS) using DHIS2

Data Analysis

Our team of highly experienced staff mix of professionals from the health and non-health sector we have extensive experience Data Collection and Extraction,Data Cleaning and Preprocessing,Statistical Analysis, Data Visualization, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Interpretation and Storytelling, Insights and Recommendations

Human Resource for Training and Capacity Building

New Organizations or organizations that have new members on their team can be trained by us in the area of health informatics and generally they can be built up capacity wise using DHIS to become skilled DHIS2 users, managers, and developers in Nigeria.
