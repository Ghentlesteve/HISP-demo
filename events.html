Events handled and executed by HISP over the years

HISP has been a major player in improving health information systems (HMIS) in Nigeria since its founding in 2004. They started by contributing to the 2006 rollout of the national software, and later became the go-to organization for the Ministry of Health. Over time, HISP became known across Nigeria for their successful use of DHIS2 as a data collection tool and their HMIS support for the Ministry and several states.
