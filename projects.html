Our Projects over the years

HISP has been in the vanguard of HMIS strengthening in Nigeria since her corporate registration in 2004. Her first major activity was to participate in the full functional roll Out of National Health Information Systems (NHMIS) software in 2006 and with further consolidation as the default backstopping organization for the Federal Ministry of Health. Since then, the organization has become known throughout Nigeria for her result oriented leadership in the deployment of DHIS as veritable tool for data collection and several other HMIS support in the FMH and a good number of states in the country.
